All About Me!

I was born in Utah, but my journey has taken me through several places that have shaped who I am.
From the sunny beaches of San Diego to the quieter pace of Granite Bay, each move brought new
challenges and experiences. Now, I’m heading to Chico, where I’ll be spending the next four years
working toward a degree in mechanical engineering — a goal that’s been fueled by both personal passion
and resilience. Growing up, I was a competitive gymnast, constantly pushing my body and mind to new limits.
That same drive shows up in everything I do, whether it’s editing videos, baking something from scratch,
playing baseball or basketball, or just laughing with friends. But a couple of years ago,
everything changed when I got really sick. Recovery has been long and difficult,
and it set me back in more ways than I expected. Still, that experience taught me a lot about patience,
strength, and what it means to keep moving forward even when things don’t go as planned. Now, I’m looking ahead with more purpose than ever. I want to earn my engineering degree and
explore the world — especially through air shows, where creativity, design, and power come
together in the sky. My story so far hasn’t been perfect, but it’s mine, and I’m proud of
how far I’ve come.
